Denver Police Data

This is the database I used to get this information Canvas. This shows Denver's police data through the month of November in 2012. I wanted to look at each arrest to see if it involved a vehicle stop or just a pedestrian stop. I did this by filtering the data to look through each precinct and if they made an arrest. I then looked at if this arrest was a vehicular stop and counted up how many there were. I then did the same for pedestrian stops getting the total as well. I now have data that shows each precincts arrest count showing if it was either a vehicular or pedestrian stop.
