Second Visualization

This graph shows the counts of wounds, diseases, and "other" injuries suffered in the Crimea war. Red is the amount of wounds, blue is the amount of diseases, and purple is the mount of "other" injuries suffered. Seems that the diseases had the highest count daily. This makes sense as this war took place in the 18 hundreds where medicine wasn't as good as it is now.
