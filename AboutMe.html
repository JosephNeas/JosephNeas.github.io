My Goal

I am currently an honor student working towards an Information Science degree as well as a minor degree in Business. I am interested to solve complex problems with data gathering and presenting them to businesses or people that would benefit from the gained knowledge. I love to look deeper into things to see how and why they are that way. I hope to use all that I have learned at the University of Colorado in order to help others through the ever-expansive and powerful tool of technology through coding.

Why I Code

I have always had an interest in coding ever since I was a freshman in high school but always put off the thought as I believed I need to start coding much younger to truly understand it. However, when college came around I wanted to study Computer Science and put my theory to the test of my belief in coding and whether or not I truly like it. My freshman in college was a very learning experience that gave me an intro to C++ and the life of coding. It was the most satisfying thing to me to get a coding project done but I absolutely hated what I was doing in C++. This lead me to change my major to Information Science, another coding degree, that focused on querying through data to find interesting and important answers in data. This is when I fell in love with coding see how I could make an impact through code and data that could be easily translated to all who read it.
